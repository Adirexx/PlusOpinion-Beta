1. Our Commitment: Transparency First

At PlusOpinion, we believe in clarity. We only use cookies that are essential for functionality, security, and anonymized performance tracking. We do not use third-party marketing or cross-site advertising trackers.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding these technologies. We maintain full compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and the ePrivacy Directive.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website function properly, enhance security, and provide an improved user experience by remembering your preferences.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize our cookies into two strict types. You have full control over non-essential cookies:

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are vital for the platform to function securely. They cannot be disabled in our systems. They manage your authenticated sessions, security protocols, and your consent choices.

Used For

Authentication, CSRF protection, and Consent management (plusopinion_cookie_consent).

Analytics Cookies

Optional cookies (Google Analytics) used strictly to understand platform traffic and feature usage securely.

4. Your Control and Consent

Upon your first visit, a banner will prompt you to accept or decline analytics cookies. You have full authority to change your preferences.

Browser Configuration

In addition to our on-site consent manager, you can configure your browser to reject all cookies or alert you when a cookie is placed. Please note that disabling "Strictly Necessary" cookies via browser settings may prevent login and access to the verified platform features.
